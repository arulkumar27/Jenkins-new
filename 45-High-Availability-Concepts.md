# High Availability Concepts

## What is High Availability?

High Availability (HA) ensures Jenkins remains available even if a server fails.

## Problem

Single Jenkins Server:

Server Failure
↓
No Builds
↓
No Deployments

## HA Solution

Load Balancer
↓
Multiple Jenkins Nodes
↓
Shared Storage

## Benefits

- Reduced Downtime
- Better Reliability
- Improved Availability

## Real-Time Scenario

Enterprise organizations deploy Jenkins in highly available environments.

## Interview Question

Q: Why is High Availability important?

Answer:
To reduce downtime and improve service reliability.
